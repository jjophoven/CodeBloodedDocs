Code Blooded began in 2017 with a group of Lake Harriet Middle School students. The team has changed over these last nine years. The team shrank to just two members in 2020-21 and has since merged with Team Progress in 2021-22 and then with CapFish in 2023-24. Since then, they have had new members join every year. For the 2025-26 season they had seven team members who are all students at Southwest high school.
